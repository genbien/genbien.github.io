Workshop

Dear Jazz Lovers!

We are thrilled to invite you to the Paris Jazz Birthday Festival – a one-of-a-kind event where we celebrate like nowhere else in the world!

Join us on October 26th for an unforgettable experience filled with incredible music, dancing, food, drinks and community spirit. Our festival promises a unique celebration, bringing together dancers from all over the globe. Prepare yourself for a day of pure joy and creativity!
